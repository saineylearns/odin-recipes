Pizza

What is a pizza?

A dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flat round base of dough baked with a topping of
tomatoes and cheese, typically with added meat, fish, or vegetables. A true masterpiece.

Ingredients List

  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic

Steps to Make

  1. Start with a solid pizza dough recipe.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Proof the dough.
  4. Prepare the sauce and toppings.
  5. Shape the dough.
  6. Bake the pizza.
  7. Cool the pizza.
