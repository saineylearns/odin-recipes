Spaghetti Bolognese

What is Spaghetti Bolognese?

Spaghetti Bolognese is a dish that consists of two main ingredients, spaghetti (the pasta)
and mince meat normally beef its a rich tomato sauce that brims with flavour.

Ingredient List

  • Mince
  • Spaghetti (pasta)
  • Cheese for the trimmings
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps to Make

  1. Cook Pasta.
  2. Cook Onions in Pan.
  3. Add Garlic and Mince.
  4. Add Chopped tomatoes.
  5. Bring all to the boil.
  6. Add pasta to the mix.
  7. Serve with Cheese.
